Classic of Mountain and Sea

Pheonix(凤凰)

Phoenix were shaped like ordinary chickens, with colorful feathers all over their bodies. They can reborn in fire

Tiger Dragon(虎蛟)

Exotic beast brings flooding wherever it appears.

Fuzhu(夫诸)

Fuzhu as a sign of great water. It looks like a white deer with four horns and is the beast that signifies water.

Taotie(饕餮)

The Taotie is shaped witg a goat head, the tiger's teeth and claws, and its voice is like that of a baby. It is a symbol of greed.

Kitsune(九尾)

Nine-Tailed Fox lives in the country of Qingqiu, has four feet and nine tails, sounds like a baby, and can eat people
